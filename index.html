Velkommen til 2021

Welcome to 2021. 2020 has been a strange year in which we have had to change plans for many of our activities. With the exception of a few months in the spring, we have been able to hold our weekly scout meetings, but it has not turned into quite a few trips and camps. We hope that 2021 will be better, at least when we get over the winter, where we can still count on living with reasonably strict restrictions.
